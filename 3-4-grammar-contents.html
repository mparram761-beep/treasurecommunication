3.4 - Vooabulary

Present Simple
This museum opens at 10 a.m.
Many tourists visit this place every year.

Past Simple
The castle was built in the 15th century.
People lived here many years ago.

Modal Verbs
You can visit the museum.
You should try the local food.

There is / There are
There is a famous monument near the square.

Comparatives & Superlatives
This is the oldest building in the city.

Question Forms
Where is the tourist office?
How much does the ticket cost?
